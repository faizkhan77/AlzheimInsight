About Us

Welcome to our innovative Alzheimer's prediction platform, where cutting-edge technology meets healthcare excellence. This project harnesses the power of advanced machine learning techniques and deep data analysis to provide accurate predictions and valuable insights into Alzheimer's Disease.

Utilizing Django, I have meticulously trained and evaluated 10 sophisticated models, including an Artificial Neural Network (ANN), on a comprehensive dataset of over 2000 patient records. Through rigorous analysis of more than 40 detailed features, I achieved an impressive accuracy rate of 97% with the final model. The performance metrics are equally outstanding, with precision, recall, F1 score, and ROC AUC all ranging between 93% and 95%.

This platform not only demonstrates the power of machine learning but also serves as a crucial tool for early detection and intervention. For those interested in the intricate details of the deep exploratory data analysis and model-building process, the full journey is documented in the link below.
